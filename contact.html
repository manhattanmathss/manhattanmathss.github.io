Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and approach.

To get started, simply fill out our contact form or call our office to schedule a free consultation. During this consultation, we'll discuss your specific needs, answer any questions you have, and recommend the best tutoring program for you. After the consultation, we'll match you with a tutor based on your academic needs and schedule.

Yes, we offer both in-person and online tutoring options. Our virtual tutoring sessions use interactive video conferencing platforms with screen sharing, digital whiteboards, and document collaboration tools to create an effective online learning environment. Many students find our virtual tutoring just as effective as in-person sessions, with the added convenience of no travel time.

We understand that schedules can change, so we have a flexible cancellation policy. We request a minimum of 24 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid being charged for the session. Emergencies are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you need to reschedule, we'll work with you to find a suitable alternative time with your tutor.

Yes, many of our tutors are available for sessions on weekends. We understand that students have busy schedules during the week, so we offer flexible scheduling options including evenings and weekends. During the initial consultation, we'll discuss your availability and match you with a tutor whose schedule aligns with yours.

Yes, we offer small group tutoring options for students who prefer collaborative learning environments. Our group sessions are limited to 3-5 students to ensure each student receives personalized attention. Group tutoring can be arranged for friends, classmates, or siblings studying the same subject or preparing for the same test. Group rates are available and offer a cost-effective alternative to one-on-one tutoring.
